This folder contains the chapter notebooks for *Python & AI for Algorithmic Trading*. Each notebook mirrors the structure of a chapter and focuses on a coherent set of ideas, moving from EMH baselines and random walks to broker APIs, streaming sandboxes, and AI‑assisted workflows.

## Chapter Notebooks

- `ch01_emh_baseline_hypothesis_testing.ipynb` — EMH as a moving baseline and algorithmic trading as hypothesis testing.  
- `ch02_practical_python_tests.ipynb` — practical EMH diagnostics on simulated and SPY data.  
- `ch03_platforms_and_project_skeletons.ipynb` — environment setup, project layout, and research skeletons.  
- `ch04_scientific_python_stack.ipynb` — NumPy and Matplotlib equity examples for trading research.  
- `ch05_static_eod_data.ipynb` — static EOD panel engineering and feature construction.  
- `ch06_eodhd_api.ipynb` — EODHD-style diagnostics and autocorrelation experiments.  
- `ch07_baseline_technical_strategies.ipynb` — SPY buy‑and‑hold and SMA crossover strategies.  
- `ch08_predictive_modelling.ipynb` — OLS predictive modelling with lagged features and diagnostics.  
- `ch09_reinforcement_learning.ipynb` — tabular Q‑learning baseline for daily trading decisions.  
- `ch10_oop_trading_systems.ipynb` — object‑oriented OLS trading system design.  
- `ch11_event_based_backtesting.ipynb` — event‑driven SMA backtests vs. vectorised benchmarks.  
- `ch12_zeromq_streaming.ipynb` — structure of the ZeroMQ tick server and message format.  
- `ch13_online_algorithms.ipynb` — online mean/variance estimators and epsilon‑greedy bandits.  
- `ch14_oanda_demo_accounts.ipynb` — Oanda-style EURUSD diagnostics using synthetic candles.  
- `ch15_ig_broker_apis.ipynb` — IG-style EURUSD diagnostics with synthetic data.  
- `ch16_reporting_monitoring.ipynb` — equity reports and position summaries for retail monitoring.  
- `ch17_docker_cloud_production.ipynb` — service specifications and compose-style deployment plans.  
- `ch18_logging_failure_management.ipynb` — logging configuration and retry demonstrations.  
- `ch19_risk_management_post_trading.ipynb` — post-trade equity curves and risk summaries.  
- `ch20_ai_enhanced_workflows.ipynb` — structured prompts and transcripts for AI-assisted research.  
- `ch21_emh_ai_retail.ipynb` — Monte Carlo EMH experiments with random‑strategy Sharpe distributions.  
- `ch22_conclusions_outlook.ipynb` — example learning plan and next‑step scaffolding.

To get started, open a notebook in your preferred Jupyter environment or in Google Colab, run the cells from top to bottom, and compare the results with the corresponding chapters and slide decks.
